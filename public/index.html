About DJWaitaminute

Based in the heart of Mobile, Alabama, DJWaitaminute is a multi-disciplinary creative force specializing in high-octane Drum & Bass, Techno, and Breakbeat music. With a background in professional audio engineering, every set is technically precise and sonically devastating.

More than just a DJ, Waitaminute bridges the gap between audio and visual arts, offering comprehensive videography and marketing services for fellow artists and event promoters on the Gulf Coast. Whether it's crafting the perfect mix or capturing the raw energy of a live show, the mission is simple: elevate the underground culture.

Resident DJ at in2GrooveRadio. Available for bookings worldwide.
